# Characters

Built with StudioBinder's **Who / What / Where / Want** `[Ep2]` and the bible's **wants + needs + catchphrase** `[Ep5]`. Drew is the **clear solo lead** — deepest arc, most space; Kai and Margo are strong support (and can occasionally drive an episode).

---

## DREW — the closer *(LEAD)*
- **Who:** faded charm, used to be the best ad man in the business.
- **What:** the agency's collapse is his personal humiliation; the discovery is his second chance.
- **Where:** the agency loft (present day) + whatever era he's pitching into.
- **Want:** the next "yes." **Need:** to matter again.
- **Hamartia (Aristotle):** *closes at any cost* — the flaw that literally triggers the antagonist (self-erasure). Visible from the first scene; detonates whenever he oversells.
- **Voice:** Don Draper — reframes desire as something he manufactured.
- **Catchphrase:** *"They didn't have less back then. They just hadn't been sold the difference yet."*

## KAI — the engineer *(support)*
- **Who:** young, brilliant, allergic to limits; jury-rigged the way into the past.
- **What:** treats history like a growth experiment.
- **Want:** scale it, ship it, more. **Need:** a limit before he gets them killed/erased.
- **Voice:** Russ Hanneman / Rick Sanchez — reckless growth bravado.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Rome ran a loss for five centuries. You scale first and patch the timeline later."*

## MARGO — the historian / continuity officer *(support; embodies the antagonist)*
- **Who:** watches the timeline and the "erasure meter"; always right, always overruled.
- **What:** the only one who reads history before they sell into it.
- **Want:** protect the timeline (and them). **Need:** to stop being ignored.
- **Voice:** Gilfoyle — deadpan, morbid certainty.
- **Catchphrase:** *"We're not changing history. We're auditing ourselves out of it."*

---

## The Want triangle (the dynamic engine)
- Drew wants Kai's portal to keep working **and** Margo to stop nagging.
- Kai wants Drew's blessing to push further.
- Margo wants Drew to listen before it's too late.
- **Drew sits in the middle** — every episode he chooses between Kai (push) and Margo (pull). That choice is the show.

---

## Dialogue voice map (with source cadence)
- **Drew = Don Draper (Mad Men).** Model: *"What you call love was invented by guys like me, to sell nylons."* / *"It's not a spaceship, it's a time machine."*
- **Kai = Russ Hanneman (Silicon Valley) + Rick (Rick and Morty).** Model: *"Amazon lost money every quarter for twenty years."* / *"To live is to risk it all."*
- **Margo = Gilfoyle (Silicon Valley).** Model: *"Your borders are merely a construct."* (flat, doom-certain).
- **Period characters (kings, courtiers) = Blackadder.** Model: *"Your brain is so minute, Baldrick, that if a hungry cannibal cracked your head open there wouldn't be enough to cover a small water biscuit."*

> The comedy comes from **clashing registers in one room**: a Mad Men ad-man, a Silicon Valley crew, and a Blackadder court.
