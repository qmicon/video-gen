# SELL-BY DATE — Series Folder

*Working title.* A 90-second vertical, episodic comedy series.

> A failing ad agency discovers it can sell modern products to the past — and races to cash in on history's untapped markets before changing the timeline too much erases them from existence.

Built under the repo's guides:
- **`screenwriter-skill/`** — McKee / Campbell / Aristotle scene craft.
- **`How to Make a TV Show playlist transcriptions/`** — StudioBinder development framework.
- **`../hooks.md`, `../formats.md`, `../spec-screenplay.md`** — the short-form vertical pipeline (format → hook → spec).

## Status (as of 2026-06-02)

**Locked (keystone decisions):**
1. **Origin / inciting incident** — *accidental discovery*: the connection opens by accident; an accidental first sale sparks the business.
2. **Antagonist** — *self-erasure through overreach*: change history too much and the crew is erased ("paradox" / timeline re-resolves without them).
3. **Cast** — three-hander, **Drew as the clear solo lead**; Kai and Margo strong support.
4. **No pilot.** Short-form series don't need one — every episode is self-contained and onboards a cold viewer in ~3 seconds. We build a strong **Episode 1** + a repeatable template instead.
5. **Dialogue Blueprint** governs all lines (see `07`).

**Drafted:** logline, controlling idea, tone, the three characters, the Dialogue Blueprint, the On-Screen Text Blueprint, and **Episode 1 ("Cold Call")** in spec format.

**Next:** finalize Episode 1, then Episode 2 ("sunscreen vs. the sun god"); assemble the one-page pitch + bible; then hand a locked script to `shotlist-builder` → `seedance-2-pro-director` for production.
