# Nano Banana Pro — 9 paired prompts (wireframe + style photos)

**How to use each prompt below:**
1. Attach the matching **`wireframe_vN_*.png`** (the geometry + labels lock).
2. Attach the **four loft photos** (the style / material / grade / mood reference).
3. Paste the prompt for that view.

The wireframe is labeled, so each prompt gives a **LEGEND** (label → what to render) plus the camera, lighting, style and bans. Render one view at a time; re-supply the four photos each time to hold the grade.

---

## SHARED LEGEND (every labeled box in the wireframes)
> `factory windows + city` = floor-to-ceiling steel-grid factory windows, blown-out daylight, hazy distant city skyline.
> `arch + painting` = rounded concrete arched alcove holding a large framed woman portrait.
> `bookshelf` = tall black metal wire shelving crammed with books and objects.
> `duct + vent` = large silver insulated HVAC duct dropping from the ceiling into a round vent.
> `sofas` = two beige/cream slipcovered sofas, slouchy and draped.
> `coffee table` = low dark-wood coffee table with ceramics and books on it.
> `desk + monitors` = cluttered black desks with white Apple cinema-display monitors glowing faint purple-blue, a silver tower PC on the floor, deep paper piles.
> `office chairs` = black leather office chairs.
> `DOOR` = a plain flush door set into the concrete wall.
> `dining table` = long dark-wood dining table with mismatched wooden chairs.
> `plants` = clusters of potted green plants.
> `nude photo (art wall)` = solid raw-concrete wall with a large framed nude photograph.
> `credenza + lamp` = low wooden credenza/sideboard with a black anglepoise lamp and potted plants.

## SHARED LIGHTING / STYLE / BAN (paste in every prompt)
> LIGHTING (match the photos exactly): single hard daylight source from the factory windows, cool ~5500K, blown-out bright glass, deep shadows with NO fill light, high dynamic range, dim moody interior.
> STYLE & MOOD (match the photos exactly): early-2000s film photography, muted and desaturated, cool grey-and-silver concrete with warm wood and beige accents, low overall exposure, visible fine film grain, soft atmospheric haze, slightly soft naturalistic focus, gritty lived-in artist-loft feel, quiet and melancholic. Not glossy, not clean, not brightly lit, not HDR-flat.
> BAN: no people, no text/watermarks/logos, no warped geometry beyond the wireframe, no fisheye, no invented rooms/doors/windows not in the wireframe, no furniture contradicting the wireframe, no oversaturated color, no clean modern render look, no rearranging the layout.
